Why This Exists

Industrial edge systems, robotics cells, and private-network sites generate security telemetry close to the physical environment. Sending every signal to a central platform can add latency, bandwidth cost, and operational blind spots. This project shows how defensive telemetry can be normalized and reviewed at the edge before heavier model runners or central security systems are introduced.
